Why New Windows?

When the time comes
to replace

Old buildings have their own unique charm — but every building needs maintenance, and windows are among the elements that require periodic replacement. When rot is too severe, damage too extensive, or performance too far gone, a new window is the right call.

Changing technology means it's also possible to improve on the past. We manufacture windows that look and feel indistinguishable from period originals — the same profiles, the same glazing bar patterns, the same hardware — while incorporating modern double glazing and draught-proofing from the outset.

The result is a window that respects the architectural heritage of your property while delivering contemporary levels of thermal efficiency, security, and longevity.

Window Types

Two window types,
one specialist team

We manufacture and install two types of window — both crafted from high-quality timber and built to match the period character of your property.

01

Sash Windows

The classic vertically sliding sash window — the defining feature of Georgian, Victorian, and Edwardian properties across Ireland. We manufacture bespoke replacements with double glazing, draught-proofing, and period-matched hardware as standard. Profiles, glazing bar arrangements, and proportions are all customisable to match your property exactly.

02

Timber Casement Windows

Traditional side-hinged casements in timber, built for properties where sash windows aren't the right fit. Available in a range of opening configurations, with double glazing and draught-proofing built in. All our casements are manufactured to your exact dimensions and finished to your specification.

03

The Process

Send us your enquiry with window dimensions and any relevant details. We'll discuss specifications, provide a proposal with a clear timeline and price, and arrange manufacture. When production is complete, our team delivers and installs at a time that suits you — with full aftercare included.
