The Problem

Old windows,
familiar problems

Aged timber sash windows are beautiful — but over time, wear takes its toll. Rattling in the wind, cold air seeping through frames, swollen or warped sashes that won't slide, wood rot around the sills, stiff or broken cords, and locks that no longer catch are all common complaints.

The instinct is to replace them. But for most period properties, restoration is not only the more sympathetic choice — it is also the more cost-effective one. Original windows, properly restored, can outperform modern replacements and last a lifetime.

Our approach is built on preserving architectural integrity while delivering modern levels of functionality, thermal performance, and security.

The Benefits
  • ✓
    Reduced Heating Costs

    Properly restored and sealed windows dramatically reduce heat loss, cutting your energy bills year-round.

  • ✓
    Elimination of Draughts

    Precision refitting and draught-proofing stops cold air infiltration at every gap and frame junction.

  • ✓
    No More Rattling

    Loose sashes and worn fittings are refitted or replaced, ending the constant noise on windy days.

  • ✓
    Improved Sound Insulation

    Tight, well-sealed frames significantly reduce external noise — particularly important in busy urban areas.

  • ✓
    Dust Reduction

    Airtight seals prevent fine dust and pollen from entering through frame gaps — a real benefit for allergy sufferers.

  • ✓
    Heritage Preserved

    Original timber windows add character and period value to a property — restoration keeps that intact.

How We Work

A bespoke solution
for every window

No two windows are in the same condition. Our process starts with understanding yours — then we develop a tailored solution with a clear plan and transparent costs before any work begins.

01

Online Enquiry

Get in touch via our contact form, phone, or email. Tell us about your property, the number of windows, and any specific issues you've noticed. The more detail you can provide, the better.

02

On-Site Consultation

We visit your property to assess the condition of each window in person. This allows us to identify all issues accurately — from frame rot and broken cords to glazing faults and hardware failures.

03

Proposal & Works

We provide a written proposal with a clear cost outline and timeline. Once agreed, our team carries out the restoration with minimal disruption — working in-situ wherever possible.
