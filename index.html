What We Do

A complete window
restoration service

01

Sash Window Restoration

Full in-situ restoration of timber sash windows — repairing broken sashes, replacing pulleys, rebalancing cords, and refinishing frames to their original condition.

02

Draught-Proofing

Specialist timber window draught-proofing using precision-fit weatherstripping — eliminating cold air, reducing heat loss, and dramatically cutting energy bills.

03

Double Glazing

Secondary and slim-profile double glazing solutions engineered specifically for period sash windows — improving thermal performance without altering historic character.
