Sash Window
Draught-Proofing

Keep the Cold Out and Your Energy Bills Down

As elegant as sash windows are, they can let a lot of cold air into your home if the right measures aren't taken. Draught-proofing is our most popular solution — highly effective, wallet-friendly, and in most cases completed in a single afternoon.

What It Does

A simple, effective
solution for every home

Draught-proofing is one of the most cost-effective improvements you can make to a period property. It addresses the root cause of cold rooms, rattling windows, and high heating bills — without requiring a full renovation or replacement of your existing sash windows.

Using precision-fit weatherstripping and replacement beads, we create an airtight seal around each frame and sash. The result is immediate: no more cold air forcing its way through the gaps, no more windows rattling on windy nights, and a noticeably warmer home.

It is an investment that pays for itself quickly through reduced heating costs — and one that protects the character and period authenticity of your property.

Why Draught-Proof?
  • ✓
    Lower Energy Bills

    Eliminating heat loss through window gaps directly reduces how hard your heating system has to work — saving money every month.

  • ✓
    No More Cold Draughts

    An airtight seal around each frame means cold air stays outside where it belongs, even in the depths of winter.

  • ✓
    Silence the Rattling

    Loose sashes are a common source of noise in older properties. Draught-proofing tightens the fit and eliminates the rattle.

  • ✓
    No Replacement Needed

    In most cases, draught-proofing delivers the performance you need without replacing a single original window.

  • ✓
    Fast & Affordable

    Depending on the size of your property, we can draught-proof your windows in as little as an afternoon — at a fraction of replacement cost.

The Process

Simple steps,
immediate results

Our draught-proofing service is straightforward and minimally disruptive. Here's exactly what's involved.

01

Bead Replacement

Parting beads and staff beads — the timber strips that guide the sashes — are inspected and replaced where worn or damaged. This restores the correct sash alignment and improves smooth operation.

02

Weatherstrip Installation

A precision-fit draught-proof strip is installed around each frame and sash, creating an airtight seal. The strip is chosen to match your window's profile exactly, ensuring the sashes continue to operate smoothly.

03

Final Check

We test every window to ensure it opens and closes freely, the seal is complete, and there are no remaining gaps. The result is a noticeably warmer, quieter room — often the same day we arrive.
